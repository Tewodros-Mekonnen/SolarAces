WHO WE ARE AND OUR MISSION

we are a group of highly creative and motivated students gather together from different higher institutions and formed a team known as Solar Aces. currently, we are participating in the L'Space summer 2019 program initiated by NASA, and we are under team-14. The current project that we are working on is building a probe that is loaded with science instruments and landing it on one of the hydrocarbon lakes (Kraken Mare) found in one of Saturns moon Titan.
